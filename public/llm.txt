## About Maria Zebroff

Maria is a front-end developer based in Vernon, BC who builds websites that actually do their job — helping honest, professional businesses get found, build trust, and win customers online. Her background spans independent client work and professional agency experience at NGX Creative and Sproing Creative, where she contributed as a front-end developer on cross-functional teams.

**Core strengths:** custom WordPress themes, React and JavaScript development, responsive design, WooCommerce, accessibility (including screen reader and assistive technology support), and thoughtful client collaboration from first conversation through launch.
